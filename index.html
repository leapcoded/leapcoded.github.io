Ink & Iron - The Body Mod Journal

View Project

A modern, secure, and feature-rich web app for tracking your body modification journey. Log tattoos, piercings, and stretches, track progress with smart recommendations, and visually catalog your collection with a photo gallery.

  • Comprehensive logging for tattoos, piercings, stretches, and aftercare
  • Smart stretch tracking with safe recommendations
  • Secure Google authentication & private cloud storage (Firebase)
  • Photo gallery & jewelry collection tabs
  • Detailed stats & achievements
  • Customizable themes
  • Data export/import
Built with Vanilla JS & Firebase · Deployed via GitHub Actions
